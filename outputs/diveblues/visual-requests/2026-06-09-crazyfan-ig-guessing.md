## B. 参考图生成提示词

### 图 1：预热图参考图提示词

```text
Create a vertical 4:5 Instagram poster reference image, 1080 x 1350 composition. The scene is a soccer game-day street poster wall at dusk, designed as a teaser for a social media guessing activity. Use a bold wheatpaste poster style with layered paper texture, tape, torn edges, subtle halftone print grain, and a high-contrast sports-event mood. The main visual should show the hashtag #CRAZYFAN partially revealed on a large poster, a generic portable handheld fan placed in the foreground or held by a young fan, and blurred silhouettes of excited soccer fans in the background. Add generic soccer elements only: a soccer ball texture, abstract team-color stripes, flags without real country emblems, scoreboard shapes, and phone comment bubbles. Leave a clean central headline area for English text: “GUESS WHAT'S COMING.” and smaller text “#CRAZYFAN KICKS OFF SOON.” The mood is suspenseful, energetic, and social-first, like the moment before a match begins. Lighting: stadium lights mixed with warm street neon. Colors: deep navy, black, bright white, electric green, yellow, and red accents. Composition: large readable headline, product visible but secondary, strong poster-wall depth, mobile-first layout with safe margins. Do not include FIFA logos, official World Cup marks, real team badges, real player faces, or copyrighted sports branding.
```

中文说明：这张用于给视觉同事参考“预热图”的氛围。重点是即将开始、悬念、#CrazyFan 露出、足球竞猜感和 Diveblues 风扇自然入画。若生图工具文字不稳定，可以先生成无字版本，让设计师后期手动加英文主标题。
