Electric vehicles                                     research identiﬁes potential socio-technical barriers to consumer adoption of EVs and determines if
Consumer attitudes                                    sustainability issues inﬂuence consumer decision to purchase an EV. This study provides valuable
Socio-technical barriers                              insights into preferences and perceptions of technology enthusiasts; individuals highly connected to
                                                      technology development and better equipped to sort out the many differences between EVs and CVs.
                                                      This group of individuals will likely be early adopters of EVs only if they perceive them to be superior in

                                                      tion policy. It can also provide guidance to EV engineers’ decision in incorporating consumer preference
                                                      into EV engineering design.
