personas.

Creative Extension Brief: From
Proposition to Idea
Now that you've crafted a strong insight and proposition, your next challenge is
to bring it to life through a creative idea — one that can drive powerful,
memorable execution across channels.
Your task is to develop:
1. A Creative Platform or Idea:
   A big, campaignable thought that brings your proposition to life emotionally
   and distinctively. It should be flexible enough to work across touchpoints but
   tight enough to feel ownable by Airbnb.
2. Examples of Creative Execution:
   Show us how the idea behaves in the world. This could include hero film, social
   content, OOH, product experience, or unexpected earned or participatory ideas
   — whatever best expresses the insight. Be inventive.
