OCOOPA Product Recall – Information for Our Partners
Dear Partner,

Thank you for reaching out and for your continued trust and support of OCOOPA.

We understand that you may have seen the recent product recall announcement and may have questions about what it means for our business and our partnership. We appreciate the opportunity to provide you with accurate information.

At OCOOPA, customer safety is not simply a product requirement—it is the foundation of everything we do. We believe that earning the trust of consumers also means accepting responsibility when issues arise. For that reason, we have voluntarily initiated this recall in cooperation with the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) for specific rechargeable hand warmer models distributed in the United States.

This recall applies only to products that meet the official recall criteria and does not affect all OCOOPA products or all markets.

Although making the decision to recall products is never easy, we believe that acting with transparency, responsibility, and integrity is the right thing to do. We would rather take proactive action to protect our customers than compromise the trust they have placed in our brand.

Since launching the recall, our teams have been working closely with the relevant authorities to ensure affected consumers receive clear guidance and timely support. We have established a dedicated recall webpage and customer support process to make the experience as straightforward and efficient as possible.

Our partnership with you remains extremely important to us. We are committed to maintaining open communication throughout this process. Should any updates affect our products, marketing materials, or cooperation, your OCOOPA contact will communicate with you promptly and provide the necessary support.

If consumers or clients contact you regarding this recall, we kindly ask that you refer them to the official OCOOPA recall webpage for the latest information and instructions. To ensure consistent and accurate communication, please avoid providing independent interpretations or technical explanations.

Thank you again for your understanding, trust, and continued partnership. We firmly believe that a company's reputation is measured not by whether it encounters challenges, but by how it responds to them. OCOOPA will continue to uphold the highest standards of product quality, customer safety, and responsible business practices as we move forward together.

Should you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Kind regards,

OCOOPA Team
