### Speed conflict resolution

`五档风速` 与 `1–100 档无级调速` 对当前 D6036 不是二选一。当前正式产品页说明：短按 `+/-` 使用五个 20% 间隔的预设档，长按进入 1–100 无级调节。旧发布会初稿只写“五档”，信息不完整，但不必直接判为错误。

### Folding and angle resolution

当前资料支持两个相邻但不同的表达：

- `180° folding`：产品折叠并形成桌面使用形态；
- `0°–180° locking`：阻尼结构在该范围内定位。

尚无证据支持“另有一个独立的 180° 仰俯轴”。对外不得把同一转轴拆成两个独立功能。

## 4. Approved Benefit Claims

### Can be used with exact model scope

- Five preset levels plus long-press 1–100 stepless control.
- 180° fold for handheld-to-desktop use.
- Four use modes: handheld, desk, neck/lanyard and integrated-carabiner clip.
- 3600mAh rechargeable battery and USB-C charging.
- Display shows speed and battery status.
- Portable, travel-ready cooling suited to commuting and everyday summer movement.

### Requires exact conditions or narrower wording

- `7.4m/s`: only for the current D6036 page carrying that exact specification.
- Runtime: current site says up to 12 hours at lower speeds and up to 3 hours at full Turbo, but test environment and laboratory method remain missing.
- `nylon + graphene`: applies to damping plates, not the whole body.
- `50:50 weight ratio`: current official tech-page claim; do not infer weight, center-of-gravity tolerance or certification.

### Not approved for new accurate product copy

- `Graphene body` / `the fan is made of graphene`.
- `5-in-1` until a current approved specification identifies five distinct use modes.
- `12-hour battery life` without the lower-speed condition and variation caveat.
- One weight or folded-size value until the current-page conflict is resolved.
- Applying D6036 facts to every Fanzy product or to D5022, D5042, N607D or another model.
- Positioning Fanzy only as wearable, women-only or light-luxury without current approval.
