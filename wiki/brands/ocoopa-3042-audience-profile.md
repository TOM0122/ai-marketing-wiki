## Core Read

The attachment points to a clear audience direction for current 3042 / UT-style products:

- 女性占比较高：UT4 Young 白、Nano 艺术、Nano 青春等分组女性占比在约 56% 到 58% 区间，UT3 lite 经典也显示女性为绝对主导。
- 核心年龄集中在 35-54：35-44 和 45-54 是多数型号的第一/第二大年龄段；部分 Nano / lite 经典分组出现 55+ 或 65+ 增长信号。
- 已婚比例稳定偏高：多个型号已婚占比约 46% 到 49%，适合推导家庭、伴侣、礼赠、照顾型购买动机。
- 高收入段有增长信号：多个型号在 `$200k+` / `$250k+` 收入段有增长或较高占比提示，但这只能作为目标人群质感和礼赠承接的启发，不应外推为整体市场购买力。
- 外观款式并非只是装饰：在 UT4 Young 与 Nano 分组中，花朵 / 艺术款销量占比明显高于基础壳款，说明“好看、可展示、可送礼”的表达有现实销售基础。

## SKU Pattern Notes

From the `销量占比` sheet:

- UT3 lite 时尚：黑底 + 黑色外壳占 Q4 销量约 77.01%，说明基础款仍承担强实用盘。
- UT3 lite 经典：白底 + 黑色外壳占 Q4 销量约 66.05%，基础款仍主导。
- UT4 Young 黑：粉色花朵黑壳占约 48.12%，高于黑色基础壳约 29.62%。
- UT4 Young 白：粉色花朵黑壳占约 49.73%，高于黑色基础壳约 23.04%。
- Nano 艺术：黄色花朵黑壳占约 63.95%，高于黑色基础壳约 17.41%。
- Nano 青春：黄色花朵白壳占约 52.26%，蓝色花朵白壳约 23.46%，紫色花朵白壳约 13.31%，灰色基础壳约 4.50%。

## Persona Implications

### 1. Style-Led Everyday Carrier

Probable source fit:

- 35-44 female-led audience
- married or family-aware
- likes useful products that still look chosen
- strongest link to UT4 Young / Nano art-style SKU patterns

Useful planning lens:

```text
她不是为了炫耀买一个电子产品，而是把一个有用的小物件自然放进包、通勤、办公室和日常穿搭里。
```

Visual implication:

- O ring / 指环 should be shown as a real carry structure: clipped to tote, key loop, coat hook, pouch, stroller, backpack strap, or winter EDC setup.
- Product should appear in hand and in context, not only as a floating product render.
- Printed / color versions can be used as accessory cues, especially in close-up stills.

### 2. Warm Gifter / Care Buyer

Probable source fit:

- 45-54 and 55+ growth signals in selected groups
- married share around 46-49%
- high-income growth signal
- strong match with gift guide, Christmas limited print, and practical care narratives

Useful planning lens:

```text
她买的不是一个“冬季小家电”，而是一份别人真的用得上的关心。
```

Visual implication:

- Show gift logic through wrapping, holiday desk, family doorway, partner handoff, parent / child care moment, office Secret Santa, and last-minute gift scenes.
- Gift scenes need the product in actual use after gifting, otherwise it will look like包装展示而不是用户场景。
- Christmas limited print should appear as a giftable version, not only as “圣诞元素贴图”。

### 3. Smart Comfort Seeker

Probable source fit:

- Not directly proven by the 3042 attachment.
- Derived from Ocoopa 2026 strategy: Sense/S6 as smart, future-facing, lifestyle / soft-wellness product line.
- Should be treated as strategy-led persona, supported by product direction and broader smart lifestyle references.

Useful planning lens:

```text
她需要的不只是热，而是“我知道它在哪里、它在什么时候帮到我、它和我的日常节奏有关”。
```

Visual implication:

- Sense/S6 should show smart behavior in real moments: app check, location reminder, left-in-bag / left-in-car recovery, commute-to-office transitions, travel temperature gaps.
- App and O ring should appear as one ecosystem, not as two disconnected features.
- The emotional tone is calm control, not sci-fi tech display.

## STP Planning Use

For the 2026 winter visualization方案, this note supports a practical STP structure:

| Layer | Recommended segmentation axis | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Segmentation | use moment: daily carry / gifting-care / smart comfort | Keeps content tied to real scenes instead of generic demographic labels. |
| Segmentation | product role: non-smart 指环暖手宝 / smart Sense-S6 | Separates “筑底实用款” from “未来感智能款” without splitting the brand story. |
| Targeting | women-led 35-54 practical lifestyle users; gift buyers; smart lifestyle seekers | Matches current attachment signals plus Ocoopa strategic product roles. |
| Positioning | a warm object you can hold, carry, show, and give | Bridges O asset, 指环暖手宝, Nice to Hold You #2, and product use. |

## Visual Rules Derived From The Data

- Prioritize real usage over feature diagrams: hand, bag, desk, coat, car, airport, doorway, holiday table.
- Make the ring visible through action: hanging, holding, clipping, reaching, passing to someone.
- Treat pattern / color editions as user identity and gift signals.
- Use plain/solid versions for reliable everyday warmth and broad practical entry.
- Do not force every persona into outdoor snow scenes; the strongest Ocoopa scenarios include indoor cold, commute cold, waiting cold, travel cold, and giftable warmth.

## Limits

- The attachment appears to describe existing UT / 3042-related products and Q4 SKU performance, not the 2026 O-ring / Sense-S6 launch directly.
- The numbers should be used as evidence for audience and visual direction, not as full market sizing.
- Persona work should combine this source with [[ocoopa|Ocoopa]], [[ocoopa-product-line|Ocoopa Product Line]], [[../campaigns/ocoopa-brand-reshaping|Ocoopa Brand Reshaping]], the existing GTM, and the 2026 winter kickoff strategy.
