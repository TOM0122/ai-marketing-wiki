# Influencer Collaboration Types

## Source

- `raw/files/IG红人建联SOP交接文档.docx`

## Free Product Exchange

The brand sends product for free and expects the creator to post content voluntarily.

Use when:

- creator is open to product-only cooperation
- account is useful for UGC accumulation
- strict content control is not needed
- cost should stay low

Main value:

- UGC content accumulation
- low-cost creator coverage
- potential long-tail social proof

Main risk:

- creator receives product but does not post

Management focus:

- shipping record
- follow-up
- output status tracking

## Paid Collaboration

The brand pays the creator for agreed content output.

Use when:

- account data is stronger
- content style fits the brand
- the brand wants more controlled or higher-quality output
- price can be negotiated into acceptable range

In the source SOP, expected price range is:

```text
USD 100-200
```

Common target:

```text
USD 100
```

Main value:

- more controlled content output
- clearer expectation on script and theme
- potentially higher quality

Main risk:

- overpaying weak creators
- accepting inflated quotes
- paying before account/style fit is clear

Management focus:

- account review
- price negotiation
- script/theme alignment
- owner confirmation before proceeding

## Practical Distinction

The difference is not only whether money is paid.

Free exchange is mainly for content accumulation.

Paid collaboration is a controlled content purchase and should be judged more strictly.
