# Workflow: Competitor Monitoring

## Goal

Track competitors, identify content and campaign patterns, and turn them into usable marketing inputs.

## Inputs

- competitor website pages
- social posts
- campaign pages
- ads and landing pages
- press releases
- product updates
- public reviews and comments

## Output

One competitor note in `wiki/competitors/` plus optional briefs in `outputs/`.

## Process

1. Collect source material into `raw/sources/`.
2. Extract observable facts:
   - offer
   - audience
   - positioning
   - content angle
   - channel
   - visual style
   - CTA
   - proof points
3. Separate facts from judgment.
4. Update competitor note.
5. Generate content opportunities.

## Output Template

```md
# Competitor: <name>

## Positioning

## Audience

## Offers

## Content Patterns

## Campaign Examples

## Strengths

## Weaknesses

## Opportunities For Us

## Sources
```
