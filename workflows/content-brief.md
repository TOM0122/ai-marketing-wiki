# Workflow: Content Brief

## Goal

Turn source material and competitor patterns into a usable content brief.

## Inputs

- target audience
- brand or product context
- source notes
- competitor notes
- user's judgment notes

## Brief Template

```md
# Content Brief: <title>

## Objective

## Audience

## Core Message

## Angle

## Proof

## Format

## Channel

## Constraints

## Draft Direction
```
