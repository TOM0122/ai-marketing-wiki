# Workflow: Instagram Influencer Outreach

## Source

- `raw/files/IG红人建联SOP交接文档.docx`
- Brand context: Diveblues

## Goal

Find suitable KOL, KOC, or UGC creators from inbound Instagram DMs, negotiate collaboration, send product, track progress, and save final content.

The operating goal is not only relationship building. The concrete output is usable social content plus a clean tracking record.

## Starting Point

Use Instagram web.

1. Open official brand account.
2. Go to `Message`.
3. Open `Requests`.
4. Screen creators who proactively DM the brand.
5. After first reply, move the conversation to `General`.

Do not keep active creator conversations in `Requests`.

## Main Flow

```text
Screen -> Outreach -> Confirm collaboration type -> Branch -> Record -> Follow up -> Save output
```

## Screening

Before replying, check the creator profile.

Look at:

- follower count
- engagement
- recent content performance
- content style
- fit with product category
- fit with brand tone

Initial classification:

- suitable for free product seeding / UGC
- potential paid collaboration
- not worth pursuing

## First Contact

The first reply should establish basic collaboration intent.

Main question:

```text
Are they open to free product exchange, or are they asking for paid collaboration?
```

This determines the branch.

## Branch A: Free Product Exchange

Use when the creator accepts product-only collaboration.

Steps:

1. Ask for shipping address.
2. Arrange product shipment.
3. Record the collaboration in the tracker.
4. Follow up after shipment.
5. Save output when posted.

Quality expectation:

- Do not over-control content quality.
- Main requirement is that the creator does not disappear after receiving product.
- Main value is UGC content accumulation.

## Branch B: Paid Collaboration

Use when the creator asks for payment.

Steps:

1. Review account data and content style carefully.
2. Negotiate price.
3. Target price range: `USD 100-200`, with `USD 100` as the common target.
4. Confirm candidate and price with owner before moving forward.
5. Discuss script / theme direction.
6. Arrange product shipment.
7. Record the collaboration in the tracker.
8. Follow up and save output.

Paid collaboration requires stricter screening because the brand is buying more control and higher expected content quality.

## Tracking

Every confirmed collaboration must be recorded immediately.

Minimum fields:

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| IG handle | Creator username |
| Account data | Follower count, engagement, useful snapshot |
| Collaboration type | Free product exchange / paid collaboration |
| Price | Final price, or `free` |
| Shipping address | Product delivery |
| Shipment date / status | Whether product was sent |
| Script / theme | Required for paid collaboration |
| Expected output date | Usually 2 weeks to 1 month |
| Output status | Not posted / followed up / posted / suspected no-show |
| Output link | Link or saved asset location |
| Notes | Special cases or follow-up items |

Core fields that cannot be skipped:

- collaboration type
- price
- shipment status
- output status

## Follow-Up

Expected production cycle:

```text
2 weeks to 1 month
```

Use the tracker to avoid forgetting creators or confusing collaboration status.

Follow-up is both output chasing and light relationship maintenance.

## Daily Checklist

- Open official IG account.
- Go to `Message -> Requests`.
- Screen new inbound creators.
- Reply to suitable creators and move conversation to `General`.
- Confirm free or paid collaboration intent.
- For paid collaboration, negotiate and confirm before proceeding.
- Add all confirmed collaborations to tracker.
- Check tracker for overdue output.
- Save posted content and update tracker.

## Escalation

Ask the owner before proceeding when:

- quoted price is far beyond budget
- content style is hard to judge
- the creator looks potentially suitable but unclear
- the creator may be disappearing after shipment
- paid collaboration reaches a reasonable price and candidate quality
